My Leadership Journey

What Inspired Me

Growing up in Kazakhstan, I witnessed firsthand the environmental challenges our country faces. The sight of improperly sorted waste and low recycling rates motivated me to find a solution that combines my passion for environmental science with cutting-edge AI technology.

I believe that technology should serve humanity and our planet. Tazalyq AI represents my commitment to creating sustainable solutions that benefit both the environment and the economy.
